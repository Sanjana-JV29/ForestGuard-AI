About This Project

ForestGuard AI is an intelligent wildfire monitoring platform that uses Machine Learning and environmental analysis to predict potential forest fire risks. The system analyzes environmental parameters such as temperature, humidity, wind speed, and rainfall to provide real-time fire risk alerts and automated emergency notification support.
